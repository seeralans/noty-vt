1.2. Concentration of Filtration

Given \((\omega, \Omega, P)\), where \(\Omega\) is the $σ$-algebra of \(\omega\) and \(P: \Omega \to [0, 1]\). A filtration is family \(\mathcal{F} = \left\{ \mathcal{M}_t\right\}_{t \geq 0}\) of $σ$-algebras \(\mathcal{F}_t \in \Omega\) such that \[ \mathcal{F}_{s} \subset \mathcal{F}_t, \quad \forall s \in [0, t) \]. Can we define a concentration in the filtration?
