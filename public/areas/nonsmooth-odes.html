Piecewise-smooth ODEs

1. Introduction

In general a piecewise-smooth ODE takes the form

\begin{equation} \label{org8c015ca} \dot{x} = \left\{ \begin{array} f^{+}(x) & \sigma(x) > 0 \\ f^{-}(x) & \sigma(x) < 0 \end{array} \right. \end{equation}

where \(x \in \mathbb{R}^d\) is the state, \(f^{\pm}: \mathbb{R}^{d} \mapsto \mathbb{R}^{d}\), are flows and \(\sigma : \mathbb{R}^{d} \mapsto \mathbb{R}\) is used to define the discontinuity surface.
